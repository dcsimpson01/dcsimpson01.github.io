Rushing Yards through October 9, 2018

Here’s a quick post using NCAA rushing data. The data was downloaded from the NCAA website on October 11, 2018.

Below is just a simple graph showing the relationship between rushing attempts and rushing yards by position. I’ll do something more interesting with the data in a future post. I also indluded the R code in case you are interested.

ggplot(data, aes(RUSH,RUSH.YDS,col=factor(POSITION)))+
  geom_point()+
  labs(x ="Attempts", y= "Yards", title = "Rushing Attempts and Yard by Position", color="Position")
