Resources

This page contains links to websites that have been helpful resources for research.

Data

The Cooperative Congressional Election Study (CCES) website has national survey data for surveys conducted before and after presidential and midterm elections. Harvard Law School maintains a helpful index of voting and polling data sources. The Roper Center archive at Cornell University stores polling data from the 1930s to the present. Voteview at UCLA maintains a database of roll call vote records and ideology scores for members of Congress.
